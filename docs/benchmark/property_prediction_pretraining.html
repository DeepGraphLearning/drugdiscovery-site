Molecule Property Prediction with Pre-Training¶

This page contains benchmarks of property prediction models with pre-training.

We have two main methods for the pre-training.

  • Self-supervised pre-training is learning the graph structural information in a self-supervised manner. Here we are pre-training on a subset of 2m molecules from ZINC15.

  • Supervised pre-training is doing pre-training on a large supervised dataset. Here we are using 456k molecuels and 1,310 tasks from ChEMBL.

For the downstream tasks, we consider the scaffold splitting for molecule data. The split for train/validation/test sets is 80%:10%:10%. For each pre-training method and downstream dataset, we evaluate with 10 random splits and report the mean of the performance.
